About me

Hi! I’m Guada, a freelance digital illustrator and computer science student. I’ve been drawing since I was 14, and art has been a part of my daily life for over 10 years. I started as a hobby, and over time it became a way for me to express myself, tell stories, and connect with others. Today, I still draw whenever I can, combining creativity, emotion, and detail in every illustration. In addition to drawing, I work in web design and development. I work as a web designer and developer using WordPress, and as a front-end developer, combining the visual with the technical to create engaging and functional experiences. I’m currently studying for a Bachelor’s degree in Systems Engineering, which allows me to continue growing and blending two worlds I’m passionate about: art and technology.
